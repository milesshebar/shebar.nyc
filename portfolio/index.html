“Duane” (2017)

COLOR (2017)

Media: Web, SMS
Size: Responsive
Exhibitions: Kenyon College Wright Center and Horvitz Hall, The Space @ The Woodward

I created this interactive webapp, which invites users to text a color (or color hexcode) to a phone number. The color they pick then becomes the color of the entire webpage. It is written in Node.js and jQuery, and uses the Twilio API to relay the texts to the server.

Visual Effects

"Backyard Wilderness" (2018)

Media: Video, Digital Composite, Motion Graphics
Size: 8K
Client: Archipelago Films, Arise Media

I performed planar tracking, compositing, and screen compositing tasks for Backyard Wilderness, delivering finals of multiple VFX shots. I also created motion graphics that were used for screen composites in the final film. In one shot, I animated an iPhone Instagram feed that responded to the actress's improvised taps, and tracked and composited it onto her prop phone.

Netflix "The Get Down" (2016)

Media: Video, Digital Composite
Size: 4K
Client: Framestore

Performed tracking and paint and roto tasks on multiple shots for Baz Luhrmann's series. In one long steadicam shot, I tracked and rotoed out modern bodega posters so they could be digitally replaced with posters from the era.

AT&T "Full Speed/Red Bull Racing" (2016)

Media: Video, Digital Composite
Size: 4K
Agency: BBDO
Client: Framestore

Performed paint/roto and compositing tasks on multiple shots for this advertisement. Worked to digitally change the paint job of one of the cars, since actual cars shot were identical. Added crowds, removed markers, tracked camera motion for set replacement.
