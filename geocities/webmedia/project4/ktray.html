Introducing KTray

Something new for the new side in all of us.
What if Gambier had a device that could hold your food?

Feast your eyes. Or just feast.

KTray is mobility-first, right out of the box. Its responsive design makes it adaptable in any dining situation, whether you're making a family-style meal or an Apple Jacks and soft-serve run. Its precision-machined semimatte border makes it a delight to hold your food. Or, at the very least, easier than desperately clutching three precariously balanced dishes and two cups of coffee as you creep towards a table at 1 mile an hour, hoping against hope you won't spill Mongolian Mac and Cheese all over yourself.
